Park Bridge

Requirements

To design a pedestrian park bridge with a span of 15 meters and a height difference of 5 meters. The main requirement for this project was to create a design that conforms with the Building Code (SNIP). Each flight of stairs can't be longer than 15 steps, all steps must be of equal height, etc.

Vision

In this project I wanted to explore the ways of using laminated timber as a sustainable alternative to steel and concrete. In order to comply with SNIP I had to do at least one landing in the middle of the stairway.

Software used: AutoCad, Photoshop
