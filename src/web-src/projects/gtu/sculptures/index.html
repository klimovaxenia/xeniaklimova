Sculptures

Padfoot

The work was inspired by English folklore about ghostly Black Hound. The vision was to create an interactive park sculpture for people to take pictures with it and children to climb, play, sit and hang around.

Estimated real height is about 2.5 meters.

The Lady

In this work I wanted to show the mystery of a young woman’s mind, her capricious character, reverie and purity.
